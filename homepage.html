About Us

WARMTH is a sand candle business that offers handmade products with creativity, customizable design, a personal touch, and decorative appeal. Since it's made of sand, we can create a variety of textures and colors that enhance the aesthetic value of our candles. This allows us to cater to diverse tastes while maintaining the charm of handcrafted artistry. A variety of images and patterns that enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space. This unique approach allows customers to personalize their candles, making each piece a distinct work of art.
